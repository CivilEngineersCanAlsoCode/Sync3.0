## Methodology Summary

This research followed a 5-phase execution plan to identify and rank the top 100 PM roles across B2C/AI/design-first companies (with B2B considered), prioritizing compensation (40%), stability & growth (30%), work mode (15%), industry alignment (10%), and work-life balance (5%). Phases 2–3 enriched and scored each company. Phase 4 ranked job platforms. All missing data was handled with stage-based defaults per the scoring rubric.
